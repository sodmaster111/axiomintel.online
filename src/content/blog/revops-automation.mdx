Shadow automation usually breaks when you skip **idempotency**. Use a `find-or-create` step and a checksum field before writing updates.

Centralize secrets in one connection owner and log every mutation to a <a href="/tools/airtable-revops-base">RevOps base</a>. Build a QA playbook with test deals that hit every scenario monthly.

Add Slack alerts with links to the exact records changed so sales leaders can self-serve context.
